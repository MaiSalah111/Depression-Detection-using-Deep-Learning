Introduction

The incidence rate of depressive among people of determination (POD) is relatively high compared to the general population. It is due to several factors such as the difficulties and the challenges they face on daily basis. This could be a serious concern to their family and loved ones and the society as whole. Depression is considered one of the most serious mental health issues and we should regard more attention to it. Hence, it is important to work around ways to help reduce the rate of depression among POD by building a fast an accurate system for early depression detection and immediately assist in finding social events and activities as a primary solution.

About The Project
